## KWS - Update Settings

URL: https://api.kws.ol.epicgames.com/v1/epic-settings/public/users/:accountId/values \
Method: PATCH \
Auth Required: Yes (`epic-settings:public:games:{game} UPDATE`)

## Path Parameters

`accountId`: Your AccountId

## Query Parameters

`game`: For Fortnite it's 'fn'
